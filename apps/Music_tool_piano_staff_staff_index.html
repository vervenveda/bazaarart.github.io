Mini Music Lessons

1. High and low: Notes placed higher on the staff sound higher. Notes placed lower sound lower.
2. Lines and spaces: A note can sit on a line or in a space. Each step moves to the next letter name.
3. Rhythm: Note shapes tell us how long a sound lasts.
4. Rests: A rest is a planned silence. Silence is part of music.
5. Compose: A composer chooses sounds and silences and puts them in an order.
