Sacred Geometry Sanctuary

Kids Quick Guide · Make your own glowing mandala

🎮 Open the Game
💭

Step 1 · Choose a Feeling

In the box at the top, type a feeling or wish like “Brave”, “Calm”, or “Loved”. Then click Manifest. A soft glowing color appears in the middle.

✨

Step 2 · Add Shapes & Colors

Pick a Theme, a Shape (circle, star, lotus, spiral…), and a Color. Click Add Layer to add a new ring to your mandala.

⚔️

Step 3 · Watch & Battle

Watch your mandala gently move. If you’d like, choose a battle shape and press Start Battle to see your mandala give you little power boosts.

You can play gently and just make art, or you can play the battle for fun. Both are okay.

Buttons in the Game

Manifest 💡 Uses your words to make a special glow color in the center.
Add Layer ➕ Adds a new ring with the shape and color you picked.
Export Geometry ⬇️ Saves your mandala as a picture file (SVG) on your device.
Clear Mandala 🧽 Erases all rings so you can start fresh.
Start Battle ⚔️ Lets your shape “fight” the computer’s shape. The log shows what happened.

Circle strong at protecting · Triangle strong at attacking · Spiral helps you heal · Lotus & Star mix bonuses

Notes for Kids & Grown-Ups

  • No log-in needed: the game runs in your browser. It doesn’t make an account.
  • Art is yours: only you choose if you want to save your mandala picture.
  • Feelings are welcome: you can type any kind feeling or hope. If a feeling is heavy, talk with a trusted grown-up too.
  • Take breaks: if your eyes feel tired, pause, look away for a bit, and stretch.
