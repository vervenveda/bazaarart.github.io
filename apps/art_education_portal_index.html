Resource Notes & Teaching Framework

This portal is built around a studio cycle: Create work, Present work, Respond through critique, and Connect art to culture, history, community, and personal meaning.

Suggested Completion Policy

For a formal classroom or homeschool course, consider completion as: 80% of selected lessons complete, one finished artwork per selected medium, one critique reflection, one portfolio export, and one certificate conference with a parent, teacher, mentor, or peer group.
