Founded February 2017

Art as a gathering place.

Bazaar Art LLC has grown into a lively center for creativity and community involvement. Across years of community work, Bazaar Art has supported immersive art exhibitions, artist showcases, collaborative workshops, and interactive gatherings designed to celebrate local talent and create meaningful connection between artists and audiences.

The Bazaar Art Zone carries that spirit forward online: more than a gallery, it is a community doorway for sharing work, discovering inspiration, and finding friendship through creative expression.
